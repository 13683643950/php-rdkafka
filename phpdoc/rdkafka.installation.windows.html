Installing the rdkafka extension on Windows

Precompiled binaries for each release are available from » PECL for a variety of combinations of versions, thread safety, and VC libraries. Extract the archive and put php_rdkfaka.dll in your PHP extension directory ("ext" by default).

Add the following line to your php.ini file: 

extension=php_rdkafka.dll
